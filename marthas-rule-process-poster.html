Martha's Rule Call Process: Old vs New

Old Process Current Workflow

1. Call Received

Martha's Rule call received by PERRT at WMS.

2. Call Handled

Call handler records details, engages with the call with minimal structure, and documents in Epic.

3. Handed Over to Local PERRT Team

All calls are passed to the relevant local PERRT team, regardless of concern type or urgency.

4. PERRT Reviews Patient

4a. PERRT reviews patient

PERRT decides how to respond and usually reviews the patient.

4b. If PERRT cannot attend

The case is handed over to the local ward regardless of urgency.

5. Outcome and Communication

Outcome documented in Epic and call log MS Forms.

6. Learning and Reporting

Data extracted from spreadsheet for reporting and learning.

New Process Proposed Workflow with System

1. Call Received

Martha's Rule call received by PERRT at WMS.

2. Call Handled

Call handler records details using the Martha's Rule Triage System for structured capture.

3. Triage and Initial Assessment

Call handler completes structured triage to understand concern and determine urgency.

4. Appropriate Route and Notifications
U1-U5 Categorisation

5. Review and Response
as required or appropriate

5a. PERRT Review

Clinician attends and reviews patient. Logged in MR patient review log system and Epic.

5b. Local Team Review

Local team responds and logs in Epic.

6. Outcome Shared and Closed

Outcome automatically cascaded to caller and team(s), then recorded for closure.

7. Data for Governance and Learning

Structured data captured in the system for reporting, monitoring, and learning.

8. Debrief Format

Debrief captured in the system using a standardised format.
